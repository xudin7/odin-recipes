What are recipes?

A recipe is a set of instructions that describes how to prepare or make something, especially a dish of prepared food. A sub-recipe or subrecipe is a recipe for an ingredient that will be called for in the instructions for the main recipe. Recipe books are a collection of recipes, help reflect cultural identities and social changes as well as serve as educational tools.
